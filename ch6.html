Video and audio conferencing

What is video and audio conferencing?

  • Video and audio conferencing is a communication method that uses both video and sound . It is a subsitute for face to face conferences, between people, who may be overseas.

  • Hardware used for this are webcams, large moniters/television screens, microphones and speakers.

    • Problems faced during video and web conferencing

  • Potential time lag in responses
  • Bad image quality caused by bad network bandwith
  • Expensive to set up
  • Whole system relies crucially on network connection

  • Network hubs are best suited for small, simple local area network (LAN) environments.

    • Advantages in video and audio conferences

  • No travelling required, faster access
  • Delegates do not need to travel to dangerous places and can easily set up the conference in their homes
